Privacy Policy for Pesabase.com

Your privacy is very important to us.

Definitions

The term "Personal Information" means any information by which your identity is apparent or can be reasonably ascertained.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

Visitors to Pesabase.com may opt-in to a subscription to a periodical newsletter; for the purpose of receiving ongoing news about Pesabase and it's various projects.

Any Personal Information collected is for the sole purpose of distributing our newsletter through our transactional mail provider MailJet. You can find a copy of MailJet's privacy policy here.

We do not collect any other Personal Information.

If you have previously opted-in, you can opt-out and unsubscribe using the unsubscribe link any time we contact you.

You can additionally request to delete your Personal Information by contacting us at contact@pesabase.com.

Security and Retention

Pesabase will protect Personal information by implementing reasonable security safeguards against unauthorized access, theft, disclosure or copying.

Personal Information is only retained for the fulfilment of it's purpose.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Pesabase reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy as required to comply with the new General Data Protection Regulations or to facilitate changes in it's business. Where possible, Pesabase will give notice of changes to this Privacy Policy.

Contacting us

You can contact us at contact@pesabase.com.
